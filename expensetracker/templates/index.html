Expense Tracker

The Expense Tracker and Analysis project helps individuals and families manage their finances effectively by recording daily expenses, categorizing them, and providing detailed insights through charts and reports. It simplifies money management by offering a clear picture of income, spending patterns, and savings. With user-friendly features, visual dashboards, and analysis tools, the system empowers users to make smarter financial decisions and achieve their budgeting goals with ease.
